About Fleet Dashboard

💡 What Inspired Me

When I joined LBS Logistics, I noticed something — we had a tracking app to see where the trucks were, but nowhere to actually store and make sense of all that trip data.

That inspired me to create this Fleet Dashboard — a place where every trip tells its story clearly, helping both drivers and companies understand and track everything day by day.

My dream is for this dashboard to grow into one of the best tools for logistics companies. In the future, I plan to build my own tracking system that integrates seamlessly — creating a complete, all-in-one experience.
